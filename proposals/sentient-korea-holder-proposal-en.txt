Sentient Korea Holder Onboarding Campaign Proposal (Final)

1. Objective
To expand the Sentient token holder community by increasing small retail holders, creating real ownership experience, and encouraging additional purchases over time.

2. Campaign Overview
This campaign is designed for Korean exchange users and focuses on behavior conversion:
Buy -> Hold -> Additional Buy -> Long-term Hold.
It is not a short-term trading promotion.

- Target Exchanges: Upbit and Bithumb
- Core Direction:
  - Lower entry barrier for first-time small buyers
  - Reward holding behavior
  - Encourage additional purchases
  - Expand the Sentient holder community

3. Total Budget
Total proposed budget: 35,000 USDT

- Operational budget: 20,000 USDT
  - Campaign operations, verification, settlement
  - AMA/content production and distribution
  - Data aggregation and final reporting

- User reward budget: 15,000 USDT
  - KRW local vouchers (priority option: Naver Point)

4. Campaign Timeline (1 month)
Week 1 — Buy Event
- Minimum required buy amount task
- Participant registration and first verification

Week 2 — Hold Verification
- Required holding verification submission
- Exclude early sellers and invalid participants

Week 3 — Additional Buy + Hold
- Encourage additional buy behavior among participants
- Mid-campaign holding status check

Week 4~5 — Full-month Hold Verification and Final Rewards
- Collect full-month hold verification
- Individual review and final reward distribution

(Operational note: 4-week campaign + week-5 verification/settlement buffer)

5. Reward Policy
- Rewards will be provided as KRW local vouchers, not token airdrops.
- Rationale:
  - Reduce mass-buy/mass-dump risk
  - Reward genuine participants
  - Improve transparency in verification and settlement

6. Verification and Anti-abuse
Given Korean exchange public API limitations, this campaign will use a proof-based verification system:

- Dedicated registration form (Naver Form)
- Buy/hold screenshots + video proof when needed
- Duplicate and multi-account filtering
- Participant database per campaign stage and final cross-check

7. KPI (Final)
1) Number of holders who bought and held on Korean exchanges
2) Total number of campaign participants
3) Number of participants who made additional purchases after Week-1 Buy Event

8. Expected Outcomes
- Expansion of retail holder base on Korean exchanges
- Conversion from simple event participation to real holding behavior
- Actionable additional-buy data for follow-up campaign optimization
- Quantitative and qualitative expansion of the Sentient holder community
